Lasagna

Description

Lasagna. . . the definitive Italian treat. This warm, luxurious bake combines noodles, sauce, meat, and cheese into the perfect dinner on a cold winter night. Follow the recipie below for the perfect lasagna in minutes. Mangia!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Lasagna noodles
  • 1 Jars of Italian Marinara Sauce
  • 1 lb ground spicy Italian sausage
  • 1 pkg spinach
  • 1 lb of shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles
  2. Cook the sausage
  3. Layer noodles, sausage, sauce, spinach, and cheese in a 9 x 11 pan
  4. Bake for 60 minutes at 400 degrees
  5. Let rest for 20 minutes, cut, and serve
